Return to Physical Activity (Educational)

- Avoid strenuous activity when experiencing chest pain, dizziness, or unexplained shortness of breath.
- After clinician review, gradually resume activity as tolerated.
- Target ~150 minutes/week of moderate-intensity aerobic activity plus 2 days/week of light resistance work, if cleared.
- Warm-up and cool-down; stop if symptoms occur.

Educational content only.
