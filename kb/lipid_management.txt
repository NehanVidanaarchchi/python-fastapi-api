Lipid Management (Educational)

- Very high LDL (e.g., ≥190 mg/dL) or established ASCVD → discuss high-intensity statin therapy with a clinician.
- For others, estimate overall cardiovascular risk and individualize therapy.
- Lifestyle: heart-healthy diet, weight management, physical activity (~150 min/week moderate), reduced saturated fat and trans fat.
- Recheck adherence, response to therapy, and side effects periodically.

This content is educational and not a diagnosis.
